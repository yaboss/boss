Vision & Mission

Yaboss is a solution from every aspect of life, from a business looking for a full time/ part time worker to a everyday person looking for a plumber or gardener to fix a problem they have, from a couple looking for a baby sitter to someone looking for a photographer to capture a special event… There is no limit to whatever you are looking for. There is a Yaboss for everyone of you

History

Yaboss was created by a group of western friends living in bali owning multiple businesses, from owning clubs to restaurants to resorts to villa managements they would always seem to go back to the same problem and that was finding workers. The idea first grew to only connect businesses and workers but living their everyday lives they realised that the same problem was not only in our workspace but also in our everyday lives. They realised that In indonesia there are millions of people that can offer services and at the same time millions of people are searching for those services but until now there has been no easy way for both partys to get into contact. From here grew the idea create and app to resolve this massive problem.
