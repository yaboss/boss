Who we are

Revolutionizing the way you do business, introducing Yaboss, Bali’s first employmentnetwork app.Stay connected with the latest job updates in your area with Yaboss. Expertly matchingprofessionals to businesses seeking skilled workers for all aspects of life.There are no limits to your pursuits.There is a Yaboss for every one of you

Vision & Mission

Yaboss was originally created by a group of expatriates in Bali as a solution for businessowners to seek reliable employees in their particular fields, however, this soon extendedfrom the workplace to everyday lives. Yaboss goes beyond your standard 9-5 job search,opening up a whole new world of opportunities right at your fingertips.Whether you’re looking for a one time job, freelance work, part time or full time employment,Yaboss keeps you connected with all of the latest job updates in your area

Name and Logo

    Just like our app, we are in the business of keeping things simple, which is how thename Yaboss was born.In the words of our fellow Indonesians, ‘ya boss’!

    Greeting you with a friendly smile, the Yaboss logo signifies leadership within abusiness, providing solutions for job seekers. The mustache symbolizes life policies thatmust be applied to achieve these goals, the glasses represent a form of boundless loyaltybetween employer and employee and the friendly smile is the face of prosperity.Yaboss personifies our employers, providing motivation and guidance in each and everyworkplace.

Workers
